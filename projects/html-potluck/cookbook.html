Classic Burger

Ingredients

  • 1 beef patty
  • 1 burger bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese slice

Instructions

  1. Cook the beef patty on a grill.
  2. Toast the bun lightly.
  3. Assemble with lettuce, tomato, and cheese.
  4. Serve hot.

Cheese Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  2. Spread sauce on dough.
  3. Add cheese evenly.
  4. Bake for 12–15 minutes.

Ice Cream Sundae

Ingredients

  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream
  • Cherry

Instructions

  1. Scoop ice cream into a bowl.
  2. Drizzle chocolate syrup on top.
  3. Add whipped cream.
  4. Top with a cherry and serve.
