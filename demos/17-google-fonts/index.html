National Mammal Rescue Day

Marine Mammal Rescue Day, celebrated annually on April 27, honors the dedicated responders, veterinarians, and volunteers who rescue, rehabilitate, and protect marine mammals like dolphins, seals, and whales. Established in 2017, this day raises awareness about threats like pollution, entanglement, and habitat loss

Overview

Marine mammals, including dolphins and seals, play a crucial role in maintaining healthy oceans. Marine Mammal Rescue Day serves as a reminder of the responsibility to protect them.
