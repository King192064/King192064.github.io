Creamy Chicken Roll Ups

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes
Total Time: 50 minutes Yields: 3

Chicken roll ups filled with Boursin, spinach, and mozzarella, seared golden on every side, and finished in a garlicky parmesan cream sauce that you'll want to put on everything. The sauce builds right in the same pan so nothing goes to waste and every bit of flavor from the sear ends up right where it should be. Serve it over pasta, mashed potatoes, or rice and dinner is done.
